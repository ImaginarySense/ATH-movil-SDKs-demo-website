ATH Móvil Web & Mobile Payment SDKs

What They Do

With our SDKs, websites and mobile app can easily integrate ATH Móvil into their payment options. These SDKs were created as posible guides for future development of ATH Móvil.

The web SDK generates QR codes which consumers can use to pay through the ATH Móvil application on their phones. The mobile SDKs allow both Android and iOS qpps to call the ATH Móvil app and complete payments. We acheived this through the combined use of the ATH Móvil API and our custom API that extends ATH Móvil's features.
